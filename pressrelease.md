### The problem: inconsistent guest experience and manual labour
Until today, hotel staff has had to update lightning scenes manually light by light, room by room. Resulting in even the same goal of lightning looking different depending on the staffer. 

### The Solution: Elegant Centralized Control
RoomLight fixes this by treating the entire hotel building as a simple programmable ecosystem. Hotel staff can create custom moods like: "welcome home", "deep sleep", "Gongratulations" on a single interface and deploy them to thousands of rooms with a single click.

### How to Get Started
Getting started with RoomLight is designed to be easy as it gets:
1. Connect the RoomLight Hub to your hotel's local network.
2. Use the RoomLight Designer app to create your custom lighting scenes.
3. Select the rooms you want and press **Sync**.

### FAQ
**How many rooms can one controller handle?**
The RoomLight system is built to scale both ways, supporting from small hotels to larger resorts with over 1,000 rooms.

**Can guests still control their own lights?**
Yes. While the "sync" feature sets the standard defaults and scenes, guests still have the ability to dim or toggle lights manually with the in-room panels.
